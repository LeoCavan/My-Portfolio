Introduction

Welcome to my profile! I am a bright and eager student from the prestigious University of Mindanao, located in Tagum City, Philippines. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) degree, which is a highly sought-after field in the ever-evolving world of technology. My passion for technology began at a young age, when I first discovered the world of computers. It was love at first byte. Since then, I have been eagerly devouring every opportunity I could find to deepen my understanding of this ever-changing field.

Recently, I completed a semester-long subject on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. While I am not yet proficient in all of these languages, I am eager to continue honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. With this foundation, I am confident that I will be able to contribute meaningfully to the field of Information Technology and make a positive impact in the world of technology.
